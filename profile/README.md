### Hi there 👋

We are based at the [University of Bristol](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/chemistry/) and are aligned with the [Atmospheric Chemistry Research Group](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/chemistry/research/acrg/). We work on chemistry-climate interactions focusing on atmospheric chemistry, climate modelling and machine learning, using [UKESM1](https://ukesm.ac.uk) and developing [UKCA](https://www.ukca.ac.uk).

#### working on

- 🥳 We are part of the Schmidt Futures VESRI [FETCH4](https://fetch4.github.io) project, on the role of methane in the Earth system, led by [Alex Turner](https://github.com/alexjturner) (UW), [Lee Murray](https://github.com/ltmurray) and Vasily Vlasenko.

- 👨🏻‍💻 We are involved in the [TerraFIRMA](https://terrafirma.ac.uk/) NERC National Capability programme (WP2), on the role of methane emissions in future climate.

- ✈️ We have started work on the ['Reducing aviation Emissions' uncertain climate impacts: NOx](https://gtr.ukri.org/project/A06B3D30-61E7-42F7-9353-DBC9F1321B34) project, looking at the climate impact.

- 🌍 We are working on DeTOX, using [GEOS-Chem](https://geos-chem.org) to study the atmosphere's oxidising capacity.
